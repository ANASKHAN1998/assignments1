I like to go to swimming in the summer .

Swimming is my most favourite activity in the summer . When the sun is shinning
and the air is warm , you will find me dipping into my backyard pool. It's not an
impressive pool, only three feet deep , but it's mine.

There are 3 reasons I like to swim:

  • I gets lots of exercise
  • I enjoy the freedom
  • I have an opportunity to be in the sun
